Our Services

If you think Home relocation, office relocation or corporate relocation is hard – you are wrong. We at Care Relocations are just for you to make your Home Relocation (Packing and Moving), Office Relocation (Packing and moving) or corporate relocation (Packing and moving) easier and simpler. With us you can make your Home Relocation, (Packing and Moving) Office Relocation (Packing and Moving) or Corporate Relocation (Packing and Moving) a cheerful experience. Care Relocations is among the most trusted organizations in the field of office, plant and laboratory relocations.

At our very first meeting itself, we make sure that we get to know what exactly you expect from us. Our survey inventory sheet is designed in such a way that it helps us in moving your shipment smoothly.

Our well-trained and experienced packing staff packs your personal effects. They use appropriate packing materials and techniques for packing your varied articles, be it a grandfather clock, upright piano or a lampshade. A supervisor monitors the entire packing process. He prepares an inventory of all the packed articles to be shipped.

An array of materials is used keeping in mind the safety of your articles ranging from heavy to light weight, sturdy to fragile. Our packing materials are quality inspected.

There will be only one coordinator through out the move of your shipment. The move coordinator interacts with our various divisions like sales, export, import, freight - sea and air, insurance, etc. and reverts back to you to answer your queries and concerns from start to finish.

Our network of relocating companies is quite experienced and is providing such services for decades. Hence, starting from premove survey to packing your valuable personal effects, custom clearing to unpack and removal of debris is done systematically.

Our Move Procedure chart details every step of the move management, be it an export or an import, leaving nothing to ambiguity. For instance, our "PRE-ALERT SYSTEM", makes sure that our destination counterpart is alerted well in advance about the move. This helps them to be fully prepared to handle your belongings as soon as it reaches the destination port, resulting in prompt delivery. The prealert system also helps our counterpart to understand the requirements of our customer and to facilitate accordingly.

We confidently assure you a quality service that will linger in your memories forever.

We move household goods as well as office effects to any corner in India. We apply our international moving expertise to domestic situation, to manage the move effectively and efficiently. From Pre-move survey to packing the goods, transporting with an escort to destination to unpack and place the goods neatly, we coordinate the various activities swiftly. We own the entire responsibility of the move. It allows you to concentrate on your personal activities, arising out of your transfer, like disconnecting the phone connection, getting a transfer certificate or new admission for your child, etc.

We are experienced in relocating industrial items, irrespective of the volume and weight. We are equipped with different types of crane and forklift facilities to handle any kind of industrial move. We can help in factory stuffing of such industrial items.

We can move any type of car, motorcycles, boats and trailers across countries. We use car trailers to move cars domestically. Wherever car trailer facility is not available, we send them through our reliable goods carrier with an escort. Also, we make sure that the vehicle is properly latched.

You must be quite concerned about moving your pet, as you won't be with them to take care of their needs, while they move from origin to destination. We do understand your concern and the importance of moving them safely and quickly. We use quality airline carriers and cages or kennels approved by such airlines. We complete all the relevant customs rules and regulations at origin as well as at destination on an emergency basis to make your pet move smooth and fast.

We offer storage facility for storing personal effects as well as office effects. Our storage unit has sufficient amount of space for all of your items. The storage unit is clean and secure. We offer door-to-door service for storing your consignment. It starts from survey at your residence, packing, transporting to our storage unit, storage as long as you wish, to deliver them when you want them back. We also arrange for storage insurance to cover theft, fire, etc. We suggest you to avoid storing valuables and antiques.

We strive for eliminating risk and ensuring peace of mind at the time of moving goods either by sea, air or land or combination of the three with loading and unloading along the way. However, sometimes the unexpected can happen. We help in insuring your goods comprehensively against any accidental damage or loss. The policy offers "All Risks" coverage from door to door. This will cover partial loss or damage of a shipment, including damage due to riots, war and terrorism at sea and in the air, and breakage, should it occur. You can arrive at the insurance value by providing detailed inventory of all your personal effects and value them at the amount it would cost to replace them at your destination. Don't undervalue your goods-if you do, at the time of settling your claim, the amount will likely be scaled down. In case of any damage or loss, when you receive the consignment, please notify our coordinator immediately. You will be given a claim form and instructions to follow for completion of your claim. We will follow up till your claim is settled.
